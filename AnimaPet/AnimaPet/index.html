About us

Who're we?

The NGO AnimaPet is an organization that offers instructions on how to create and adopt a pet. AnimaPet promotes pet adoption events and donations to be redirected to animals living in kennels or on the streets. The purpose of our work is: To raise doubts about adoptions and donations. Disseminate NGOs and websites that seek to help abandoned animals. Share news about Pets. Instruct our readers how to take care of their Pets.

Adoption

Why adopt a pet?

If you want to adopt a pet and contribute to our work of continuing to rescue animals. Please schedule a visit at our institution, both to adopt or even make affection for the animals, contact us wherever you prefer. Most of the time, we rescue animals that have been abandoned or suffers abuse. Therefore, they will be much more affectionate with their owners, developing a great sense of fidelity and gratitute. Thanks for that.
